Refund Policy - Brew&Bean Coffee Shop  

1. Refunds are available only for online pre-orders made through our website or official WhatsApp channel.  
2. In-store purchases are not eligible for refunds, but customers may exchange items within 7 days with a valid receipt.  
3. Refunds for pre-orders will be processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.  
4. Catering orders must be canceled at least 48 hours in advance for a full refund.  
5. Gift cards and loyalty rewards are non-refundable.  

For further assistance, please contact support@brewandbean.com.
